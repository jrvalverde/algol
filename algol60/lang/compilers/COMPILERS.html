    Course's Final Works

    1995

    • SmallTalk to Delphi Translator. Jimenez, Julian, et al.
    1996
    • LBOP - A multitarget (PC & Macintosh) compiler optimizer. Biazutti, Ola & Petersen.
    1997
    • Kakuy - Chapin (or  Nassi-Schneiderman) diagrams from ANSI C programs. Colman, Villalba & Vittar.
    • InspectorC - A tool that generates white box test cases and information (like ciclomatic complexity, call tree, variable usage, etc.) from ANSI C programs. Argañaráz, Cavadini & Tomasini.
    1998
    • Grammar Editor. Gola, Hernández & Paiola.
    • Software Metrics for Java programs. Jiménez & Teseyra.
    • CFGs and Dominator Trees Generator for multiple languages (C, Pascal, BASIC & Modula 2). Kurrle
    • Pascal to POV translator.
    • Pascal to LBOP's cuads translator.
    1999
    • Turbo Pascal to Intel 8051 and Microchip PIC assemblers compiler.
    If you want more information about any of these works please mail to compiladoresucse@hotmail.com
